Privacy Policy for Plated: Recipes & Groceries
This Privacy Policy outlines how Plated: Recipes & Groceries ("the App") handles your information. Your privacy is a priority, and the App is designed to be as minimal as possible regarding data collection.

1. Data Collection and Usage

Plated: Recipes & Groceries does not collect, store, or share any personal data.

No Accounts: You are not required to create an account or provide an email address to use the App.

No Tracking: The App does not track your behavior, location, or usage patterns.

No Third Parties: We do not use third-party analytics (like Google Analytics) or advertising services that could collect your data.

2. Local Storage

All app usage happens locally on your device. Any recipes you create, grocery lists you build, or preferences you set are stored on your phone and are not uploaded to any external servers.

3. Email Communication

If you choose to contact us via email for support or feedback:

Your email address and the content of your message will be used solely to respond to your inquiry.

We do not sell, rent, or share your contact information with any third parties.

4. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.
